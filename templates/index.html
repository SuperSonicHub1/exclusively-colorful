Exclusively Colorful

Made by Barbara Odae, Emmanuel Olusheki, Kyle Williams, Tomilola Adegoke

Problem: Black and Latinx NYC students attending private boarding schools want access to a large and diverse network of POC peers that promotes inclusivity and intersectionality where they can connect and relate to each other in a casual setting during their time in high school

This idea was born out of inspiration for the Black at instagram accounts and as a group project. Exclusively Colorful is designed to be a platform that will provide a safe space for black and brown NYC students attending boarding schools through the use of:

    • Forums with a vast selection of topics that students can have casual conversations and unwind over
    • A system where students can share their difficult racially targeted experiences in their boarding schools
    • A page of resources for users to reference for whatever they deem fit
