Exclusively Colorful

Where Students of Color Can Express Their True Colors


THE PROBLEM

Black and Latinx NYC students attending private boarding schools want access to a large and diverse network of POC peers that promotes inclusivity and intersectionality where they can connect and relate to each other in a casual setting during their time in high school.

THE SOLUTION

Exclusively Colorful is designed to be a platform that will provide a safe space for black and brown NYC students attending boarding schools through the use of:

  • Forums with a vast selection of topics that students can have casual conversations and unwind over
  • A confession outlet where students can share their difficult racially targeted experiences in their boarding schools
  • A page of resources for users to reference for whatever they deem fit
